Store compliance baked into these screens

  • Screen 3 (paywall): price, period, auto-renew disclosure, Restore button, Terms + Privacy links — all required by Apple.
  • Screen 5 (account): Sign in with Apple shown first (required if other social logins are offered on iOS).
  • Screen 7 (subscribed): Manage Subscription link (Apple/Google), Delete Account button (both stores require in-app deletion).
  • Screen 9 (lapsed): Download cloud data option honors GDPR/CCPA data-export right even after lapse.
  • Not paywalled: account creation, deletion, data export — these stay free even after subscription ends.
